FlyTo

Are you flying somewhere? Plan your journey using FlyTo! Make a detailed plan of your journey and never worry about being late again!

  • Add your flight and ticket information
  • FlyTo will create a recommended journey for you, including arrival time based on the airports, maps, etc
  • Adjust the journey to your liking, add extra details and information like dining info and other purchases
  • Don't forget about that cup of coffee!
  • Find a taxi or metro/tram/bus tickets
  • Add your booking info and connect with a return trip
  • FlyTo will generate a journey for you that will auto-update, all you have to do is follow the plan
  • In case of delays or other unexpected things, FlyTo will quickly adjust and help you maintain the plan
